About Us
At Galway Instruments Store, we are passionate about helping musicians find the perfect instruments for their needs. Our Store was first established in 2003 by brothers John and Tony King. The brothers grew up surrounded by music in the house, with their grandfather Paddy King being a well known musician in Galway and their mother being a bandmember of local band Swirl.
After finishing school, John headed to America where he worked with skilled instrument makers, Tallys. Here he gained a wide knowledge about instruments and gained valuable experience. After 10 years there, he decided to return home to Galway and set up Galway Instruments store along with his brother Tony.

Our mission is to provide high-quality instruments at the best prices, while also offering exceptional customer service. We believe that everyone should have access to great musical equipment, regardless of their skill level or budget.
Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned professional, we have something for everyone. From guitars and drums to keyboards and amplifiers, we carry a wide variety of instruments to suit your needs. Our team is knowledgeable and passionate about music, and we are always happy to offer advice and guidance to our customers.
Our website is updated as frequently as possible but it you don’t see what you’re looking for, just get in touch!
